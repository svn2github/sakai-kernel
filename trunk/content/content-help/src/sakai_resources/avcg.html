Deleting a Resources item

To delete an item from Resources, follow these steps:

  1. To delete one item at a time, mouse over Actions, and then click Remove. To delete several items at a time, mark their corresponding checkboxes, and then, above the list of items, click Remove.

  2. To confirm that you want to delete the item(s) listed, click Remove. Or, to cancel, click Cancel.

Note: You can't delete a folder that contains an item. You must remove (i.e., move or delete) its contents first before deleting a folder.
